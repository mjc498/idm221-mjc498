I share my home with two affectionate feline companions, Cal and Boots, both of whom are three years old. We welcomed these loving brothers into our family after adopting them from a farm owned by my sister's friend.Transitioning from stray barn kittens to cherished members of our domestic life, their antics never fail to bring joy and amusement into my daily routine.

Despite my monthly visits home, I've noticed a decline in my tolerance for dander. Armed with allergy medication, nothing can deter me from indulging in the comforting embrace of my cats, using them as delightful fluffy pillows for nighttime cuddles.
